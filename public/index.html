About

Vivekananda College of Engineering and Technology has successfully trained and guided multiple students towards excellency. For the growth of students who are young aspiring researchers and engineers, a healthy, competitive environment is necessary which was observed to be missing in the institute. Thus the CoRE was founded.


CoRE has the following aims

  • Provision of healthy, competitive environment for growth of young researchers and engineers.

  • Moulding students into “True Engineers”.

  • Development of technical, communication, and documentation skills.
